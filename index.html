Help:
  • 'Full Dungeon' will give a mix of vocab. types, Noun Dungeon is...only Nouns!
  • Use the arrow keys to move, and the 'Enter' key to use something.
  • One ghost per level carries the stair key.
  • Chests are denoted as a * symbol. They might contain useful items!
  • Stairs are denoted as a < or > symbol. You'll need a key before you can use them.
